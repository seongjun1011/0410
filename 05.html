Google refers to an American multinational IT company that operates a web search, portal site, or platform and uses advertising as its main revenue model, or a search engine serviced by Google. It is the overwhelming number one search engine in the world, a multinational company celebrating its 20th anniversary as of 2018, and one of the largest internet companies in history and is a member of MAGA along with Microsoft, Amazon.com and Apple.

On August 11, 2015, with the transition to a holding company system, Alphabet was newly established and became its subsidiary.
